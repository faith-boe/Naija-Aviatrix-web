NAIJA AVIATRIX

Sisters United

Naija Aviatrix originally named Fun Loving Flying ladies was created and formed as a WhatsApp group by Captain Mercy Odushola Eghonghon in November 2014. These group of Nigerian female pilots united by their desire to break boundaries and change the norm are not only fun loving lady aviators, they have united against all odds to bring change, aid, awereness and support to all around them. Over the years, these ladies have contributed to raising funds from within themselves for those in need and they did not stop even through the hard times of the corona pandemic.

Our project, running yearly on the week of The International day of The Girl Child, aims to bring girls together from all ethnicity and background to meet and greet women thriving in their various fields. Activities planned is set to motivate and inspire girls to believe in their dreams and aspire to achieve nothing less than their best. To support and sponsor one of these events, contact us.

We have a big heart for others, our members are commited to inspiring and motivating the future generation. Our kind hearted generosity and intergrity in helping the needy, is what has lead to the growth of our membership to over seventy female pilots. We do not intend to stop here. A lot of our members have experienced the same tough times and hardships we face in our economy and therefore know first hand how to help you navigate your way through whatever journey life may take you.

We intend on continuing the good work by offering mentorship to boys and girls alike. With partnerships and sponsors, we would be able to continue to do more and reach even farther.
